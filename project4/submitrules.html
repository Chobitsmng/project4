Rules for the Contest

Contest Rules
  • Submissions must be in .png, .jpg, .jpeg, or .gif file format and no more than 400 x 400 in size.
  • Please include the topic you are submitting to in the file name you are uploading.
  • Submissions Must be submitted by the 25th of every month by 11:59pm eastern time.
  • Voting lasts from the 26th at 12pm until the 30th at 11:59pm eastern time.
  • You can vote only once per month in each topic.
  • Any one may share on social media to get more votes. Advertise yourself!
  • Winners for the previous month are posted in the winners gallery by end of day on the 1st of every month.
  • Submitters are allowed to enter in to one category per month.
  • If you are declared a winner you are not allowed to enter the contest again for 2 months. This is to make it more fair so that others have a chance to win.
  • Submitters must live in the Detroit metro area.
  • Winners have 24hrs after notification to claim their prizes and verify thier contact information.
  • Prizes are sent vis U.S. Mail within 5 business days winners info verification.
  • For any questions about the rules or submitting your photo please contact us via the mail or social media buttons on the bottom of this website.
  • And of course have fun!!
